## About CTS

**Creative Thinking Systems** is a European research and advisory group focused on AI and creativity. Founded by Conor Roche.

**Credentials:** MSc AI (Bath), BSc CS & Maths (UCD). 20+ years building creative operations from zero. Currently Sector Head for Entertainment & Culture at NEOM ($50–100M budget), transitioning to full-time CTS in August 2026. Previously Director of Innovation at BOP Consulting, founded BOP's Asia operation (Shanghai, 6 countries). Board member, Gate Theatre Dublin. Nesta/Clore Fellow.

**Key differentiator:** CTS bridges EU regulatory expertise (EC research, AI Act, Apply AI Strategy) with Gulf operational experience (NEOM, city-scale AI deployment). Neither large consultancies nor specialist tech vendors span both.

**Technical partner:** Disrupter ([disrupter.com](http://disrupter.com)) — engineering capacity and co-delivery on sovereign AI and product work.

## Three Pillars

### 1. Advisory & Operations

AI strategy and implementation for cultural institutions, sovereign AI advisory, AI-enabled venue operations. Day rates €2.5–3.5K (EU) / €3–5K (MENA).

**Three consulting offers:**

**AI Strategy for Cultural Organisations** — Assessment of current AI readiness, opportunity mapping across operations/programming/audience, governance framework, implementation roadmap. 2–4 week engagements. Target: museum directors, arts council leaders, cultural foundations.

**Sovereign AI Advisory** — Data sovereignty architecture, governance frameworks, vendor independence strategies, EU AI Act compliance. Retainers or projects. Joint delivery with Disrupter. Target: government cultural authorities, sovereign wealth funds, national institutions.

**AI-Enabled Venue Operations** — Scheduling optimisation, workforce planning, dynamic pricing, predictive maintenance, programming intelligence. Primarily MENA. Target: entertainment operators, cultural venue managers, developers.

### 2. Research & Development

**Demonstrator projects:**

- **Dead Internet Monitor** ([deadinternetmonitor.com](http://deadinternetmonitor.com)) — Live application tracking AI-generated content and bot activity across platforms. Built and deployed. 5 microservices on Cloud Run.
- **Casios** ([casios.io](http://casios.io)) — Cooperative artefact engine where multiple frontier LLMs cooperate to produce shared artefact bundles. Spec complete, prototype pending.

**Applied research:**

- **Cooperative Intelligence** — Evaluation frameworks for measuring cooperative capabilities of AI agents. Four components: Cooperative Reasonableness Index, Arena, Protocol, and Agent product.
- **Agent Culture** ([agentculture.ai](http://agentculture.ai)) — The norms, knowledge, artefacts and collective behaviours of agents as they interact with each other over time. Connected to cultural evolution theory.

**EU funding pipeline:** Horizon Europe, Creative Europe, Digital Europe programme calls. Strategy for AI in cultural/creative sectors expected H1 2027.

### 3. Products & Media

- **Moonbeam** — Repositioned as deployable B2B content supply system. Autonomous media venture generating, publishing, and monetising content.
- **Casios** — Also a product play beyond the demonstrator.
- **DIM data products** — Newsletter, data reports, API access to bot/AI content tracking.
