# **Creative Thinking Systems**

**Applied AI for Cultural and Creative Industries**

Web Copy · v0.1 · March 2026

---

**Creative Thinking Systems** is an applied AI research, development, and production entity focused exclusively on cultural and creative industries. We exist to ensure that AI serves creativity rather than displacing it, and that the people, artists, creatives, and institutions who create culture retain agency, ownership, and economic reward in the process.

Our mission is to enhance, incentivise, and enshrine human creativity in an era of artificial intelligence.

---

## **What We Do**

We collaborate with cultural institutions, venues, creative organisations, and public bodies, through work that spans research, strategy, and production.

### **Research & Development**

Applied research and public demonstrators that make the fast-moving AI landscape legible for the cultural sector.

* **Dead Internet Monitor (DIM):** A live diagnostic tool tracking the proliferation of AI-generated content and bot activity across social platforms — measuring how "dead" the internet is becoming  
* **Copyright Intelligence:** Monitoring how AI systems crawl and train on cultural and copyrighted content — tracking crawler compliance, and developing attribution methodologies and systems  
* **Cooperative Intelligence Benchmark:** Evaluating and informing how frontier AI models cooperate with humans on creative tasks

### **Strategy & Operations**

AI strategy, design, and deployment for cultural organisations — from readiness diagnostics to sovereign infrastructure.

* **AI for Cultural Organisations:** Diagnostic health checks, workflow design, training, and implementations tailored to the specific needs of cultural institutions adopting AI.  
* **Sovereign AI:** Deploying AI solutions that keep sensitive content, data and workflows under the organisation's own control — built on open-weight models and nationally compliant infrastructure.  
* **AI-Enabled Event Production:** Sovereign agentic systems that coordinate event production

### **Production & Content**

AI-native production capabilities

* **Autonomous Content Production:** AI-driven content creation across multiple formats and platforms, from creation to distribution  
* **World Models for Culture:** Applying emerging AI world models — systems that generate coherent, navigable environments — to cultural production: immersive venue experiences, interactive heritage walkthroughs, and virtual reconstructions of historical sites and performances  
* **Generative AI for Public Space:** Culturally authentic generative content and music for venues, public spaces, and cultural districts — serving regional aesthetics at scale
